Get AP scores ASAP

Can't wait for them to come out? Pretend you live on the West Coast and get them on Tuesday, July 5.

See how

1. Get a reputable VPN

They can be free or paid. Be careful in selecting your VPN service. Make sure it has servers in California or Washington state.

2. Download and initialize VPN software

Make sure it works before you try on Tuesday, July 5.

If you're super paranoid, try to get multiple VPN services. There is more than one way to get into California.

3. Wake up on the morning of AP scores

Depending on where you live, wake up before this time and log in to APscore.org at this time:

  • Eastern Time: 8am EDT
  • Central Time: 7am CDT
  • Mountain Time: 6am MDT
  • Pacific Time: 5am PDT

4. Connect to the VPN service

Make sure the server is physically located in California or Washington state. Cities:

  • Los Angeles
  • San Francisco
  • Seattle
  • Portland
  • San Jose
  • San Diego

5. Go to APscore.org

The website will be extremely laggy but bear with it. Everybody else is also rushing to get their AP scores.

If the website detects you're not a "legitimate" IP address, just try again with another VPN. The paid ones tend to work the best, since they are less abused.

That's it! Good luck.

Best of luck to you this AP Score Checking Season.
